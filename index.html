 BRING NATURE

INDOORS

Though we cannot see the health threats surrounding us everyday,

the good news is we are not entirely defenseless against them.

Through the use of Cold Plasma Technology, Aller is focused on creating

products that can efficiently and effectively sterilize the air we breathe

indoors and the things we use everyday.

All Aller Plasma Sterilizering products artificially produces “cold plasma” – an

unstable mixture of negative and positive ions to neutralize and eliminate

harmful toxins in the air by breaking down their molecular structure.
